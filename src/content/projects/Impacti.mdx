The Pro Planet app faces significant usability and engagement difficulties in areas like product recommendations, sustainability tips, community interaction, and personalization. Its gamification elements and educational content lack effectiveness in keeping users engaged, while tracking functionalities fall short in providing a smooth experience. These combined issues reduce the app's impact on promoting sustainable habits. Improving user interaction and feature integration is crucial to fostering long-term sustainable lifestyle changes.

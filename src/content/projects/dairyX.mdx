DairyX simplifies the milk collection process by allowing users to select suppliers, record collection details, and monitor milk quality with a visual representation. The app provides a clear overview of total milk collected, recent pickups, and the ability to filter data by date, supplier, or quality. Users can access detailed reports with charts and download receipts or bills. A dedicated receipts and billing section ensures smooth management of transactions.
